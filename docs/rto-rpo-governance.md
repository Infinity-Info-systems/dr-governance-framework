# RTO / RPO Governance

## Purpose

RTO/RPO governance defines how recovery targets are set, reviewed, approved, and tested.

## Governance Practices

- set targets by criticality tier
- review targets on a fixed cadence
- validate targets through testing
- escalate unrealistic targets

## Questions To Ask

- are the targets realistic?
- are they aligned to business need?
- are exceptions documented?
- are they being tested?

## Evidence To Collect

- target register
- approval record
- test results
- exception log
