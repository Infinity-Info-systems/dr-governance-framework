# Recovery Approval Workflow

## Purpose

The approval workflow defines how recovery targets and recovery designs move through review and sign-off.

## Workflow

- assess criticality
- propose recovery tier
- review RTO/RPO
- approve or reject
- track exceptions
- record review evidence

## Output

- approved plan
- conditional approval
- exception
- follow-up action

## Use

Use this workflow to keep recovery targets and plans moving through review with traceability.
