# Recovery Tier Model

## Purpose

The recovery tier model maps criticality to recovery intensity, test depth, and approval rigor.

## Example Tiers

- Tier 1: immediate recovery
- Tier 2: rapid recovery
- Tier 3: scheduled recovery
- Tier 4: best-effort recovery

## Use

- align RTO/RPO to business needs
- determine validation cadence
- explain the recovery intensity expected for each workload tier

## Outcome

The tier model should translate business criticality into the right recovery rigor and response expectations.
