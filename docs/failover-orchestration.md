# Failover Orchestration

## Purpose

Failover orchestration describes how recovery actions are coordinated during an outage or planned exercise.

## Orchestration Areas

- detection
- decision
- execution
- validation
- communication

## Questions To Ask

- who leads failover?
- what steps are manual?
- what is automated?
- how is success confirmed?

## Evidence To Collect

- orchestration runbook
- communication plan
- validation evidence
- rollback plan
