# Business Continuity Alignment

## Purpose

Business continuity alignment connects recovery design to business priorities, critical services, and continuity expectations.

## Alignment Areas

- critical services
- business impact
- recovery expectations
- continuity dependencies
- regulatory expectations

## Questions To Ask

- what must recover first?
- what is the business impact of downtime?
- what dependencies affect continuity?
- what is the acceptable recovery window?

## Evidence To Collect

- continuity plan
- critical service map
- dependency notes
- business impact assessment

## Use

Use this page to show how recovery design supports real business continuity priorities.
