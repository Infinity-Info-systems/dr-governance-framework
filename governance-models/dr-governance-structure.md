# DR Governance Structure

## Purpose

This structure defines who owns recovery decisions, who reviews the plans, and how exceptions are escalated.

## Roles

- DR lead
- application owner
- infrastructure owner
- business continuity owner
- executive sponsor

## Operating Rules

- every critical workload has an owner
- recovery tiers are approved
- exceptions are time-bound
- tests have documented outcomes
- approvals are traceable to a named reviewer

## Governance Outcome

The structure should make recovery decision-making clear, timely, and evidence-based.
